Monthly Temperature Trends: A Year in Review Across 10 U.S. Cities (2014-2015)

By: Trushaa Ramanan | INFO 474 Final Deliverable

This visualization displays weather trends for 10 U.S. cities from July 2014 to June 2015. The chart shows the distribution of actual average temperatures for each month using density curves. Hover over each month to see the average, minimum, and maximum temperature for that month. Simply move your cursor over the data point to reveal the tooltip with the temperature information.
